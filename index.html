1. Go to your profile
2. Touch on (right top) menu icon
3. Touch "Delete Account" button
4. And confirm that you are going to delete your account
5. If operation run without error (connection issue or something else) your account will be deleted and you will be navigate to login page
With this account deleting, your name, username, email, posts, pictures, friends, likes and dislikes will be deleted completely
