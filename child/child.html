Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)
We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation on our platform.
Our app does not allow the sharing or hosting of user-generated content that depicts, promotes, or glorifies CSAE in any way.
We:
Use content moderation and reporting tools to monitor and review content.
Immediately remove any content that violates this policy.
Report CSAE content to appropriate legal authorities and organizations, including NCMEC.
Provide tools for users to report abuse directly in-app or by emailing [your support email].
If you become aware of content that may violate this policy, please contact us at
